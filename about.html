about the brand

In 1925, Bjell Borgen, a visionary in Östersund, Sweden, founded the iconic clothing brand "Bjell." Inspired by the natural beauty and spirit of Sweden, Bjell aimed to blend timeless elegance with individuality. Known for meticulous craftsmanship and exclusive wool, the brand quickly became synonymous with quality and authenticity. Today, Bjell continues to honor its rich heritage, offering contemporary fashion that echoes Bjell Borgen's enduring vision.
